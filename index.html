About KYTC

The overall goal of the educational programs and other activities covered by the Khan Yunis Training College is Palestinian refugees from the age groups from 14 to 22 years in the Gaza Strip in general and in the southern region in particular, to overcome the consequences of the disengagement and the Israeli withdrawal in Social and economic fields, by providing targeted age groups with education and vocational and technical training in order to obtain packages of competitive educational and training skills that help them more efficiently tocontribute to the development of the local community in the Gaza Strip and the region.
